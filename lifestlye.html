Lifestyle of a Traveller

Sometimes in life you dont know which road leads to where, the best way to find that out is to just run along the way and make down to that road cause that path may lead you to a bundle of joy , a bag of sorrow or a loadfull of both. But the thing is if you won't go down that road you will always have that incomplete part left inside you asking you every day what would have happened if went for it and belive it's the worst part it makes you feel incompetent of yourself and a bit no self confidence person. So next time when there is a choice of going down the road to figure out what would happen take it because it will make you strong for the next stone thrown towards you.
